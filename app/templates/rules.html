Rules

Predictions:

- Predict the outcome of each game before its scheduled start time.
- Prediction should be the final score (after overtime and penalty shots).
- There are no draws possible in play-offs.

- For bonus point, predict which team will score first.
- Predictions can be altered until the scheduled start time.

- After the game has started, predictions from all players will be locked and published.

- After the game has finished, earned points for all players will be published and overall standings updated.

- In your profile page you can predict the winner of the competition.
- Winner of the competition can be changed throughout the tournament.
- Points awarded for the correct prediction of the winner decrease as the tournament progresses.*

*Prediction made before the start of tournament are worth most points (15), predictions made before the final are worth least points (1). See full breakdown below.

Scoring:

- Correctly predicted outcome – 2 points.
- Correctly predicted outcome with correct goal difference – 3 points (2 + 1).
- Correctly predicted score – 6 points (2 + 1 + 3).

- Correctly predicted first goal – 1 point (no matter which team wins).
